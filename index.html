ShareTea Redmond

Established in 1992, ShareTea started off as a Take-Out business for black tea and pearl milk tea drinks in Taipei, Taiwan. Since then, ShareTea has opened many branches and franchises in 18 countries such as the United States, UK, Australia, Singapore, Indonesia. They currently have over 450 stores.

The newest location opened up in Redmond, Washginton in March 2017.
